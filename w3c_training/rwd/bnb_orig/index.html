LOW COST ROMANTIC VENICE

Our Bed and Breakfast is located in a typical Venetian area, on the second floor of a Venetian palace,
with private entrance and canal view, close to San Marco Square.

The neighbourhood is very beautiful and characteristic, the house is just a footstep away from the most important monuments, churches and shops,
with restaurants, pizzerias and bars around the corner.

Take a look at our comfortable and elegant rooms. Each bedroom has a seating area and balcony overlooking the canal.
