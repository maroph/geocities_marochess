Introduction

This site is mainly my HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript client-side playground. No server-side dynamic code is used on this site. All pages are validated with the W3C Markup Validation Service and the W3C CSS Validation Service. This site doesn't even try to support older browsers, so don't expect much fun with a browser older than Internet Explorer 9!

History of My GeoCities Site

Way back in 1996 I created my GeoCities site. It survived the change of ownership to Yahoo! - and to my surprise even the shutdown of the Yahoo! GeoCities service. By chance I learned in June 2012 that the GeoCities sites are backuped and still available under the slightly changed URL geocities.ws (see the Geocities.ws Web Archive Project page for more details). On June 30th 2012 I activated my old account again.

History of the URLs of my GeoCities site:

  • http://www.geocities.com/CapeCanaveral/Launchpad/2640/
  • http://www.geocities.com/marochess
  • http://www.geocities.ws/marochess
